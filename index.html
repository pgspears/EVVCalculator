Billing Calculator

Enter service start and end times below to calculate the elapsed minutes, raw units, billable units, and billable amounts. The calculator normally computes the billable units automatically based on the time inputs. However, if you prefer to enter the Billable Units manually, click the "Manual" button in the Actions column to toggle manual mode. Once enabled, simply type a value (between 0 and 96) into the Billable Units field, and the Billable Amount will update automatically based on the unit rate.
